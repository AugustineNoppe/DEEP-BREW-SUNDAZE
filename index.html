Who We Are

A progressive take on Southern African nightlife

DEEP BREW, Sundaze celebrates a progressive take on Southern African nightlife — inspiring cosmopolitan cultural diversity and romance with sound.

Originally founded in June 2014, our Sunday Tradition began on the 6th of December 2015 at Gqeberha's iconic Roof Garden Bar. A carefully curated blend of friends, food, music and drinks.

Trust us — there's no better place to be on a Sunday.
