Who We Are

A progressive take on South African nightlife

DEEP BREW, Sundaze is South Africa's favourite rooftop Sunday party experience — celebrating cosmopolitan cultural diversity and romance with sound in the heart of Gqeberha.

Founded in June 2014, our Sunday Tradition began on 6 December 2015 at Gqeberha's iconic Roof Garden Bar. We bring together friends, food, curated music and drinks every Sunday.

Whether you're chasing the perfect sundowner or looking for the best rooftop event in the Eastern Cape — there's no better place to be on a Sunday.
