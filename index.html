Our Story

A Sunday tradition since 2014

What started as a small gathering on a rooftop in the heart of Gqeberha has grown into one of South Africa's most loved Sunday experiences. Deep Brew Sundaze is more than a party — it's a ritual.

Every week, the Roof Garden Bar transforms into a sanctuary of sound, flavour and connection. Carefully curated DJs, a handpicked bar menu, and the warmth of the Eastern Cape sun make every Sundaze unforgettable.
